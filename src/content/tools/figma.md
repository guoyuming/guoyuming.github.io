## What It Does

Figma is the industry-standard collaborative design tool, now enhanced with AI capabilities. It enables teams to design, prototype, and hand off interfaces in a shared environment. Recent AI features include first-draft generation, smart rename, visual search, and content generation.

## Key Features

- **AI First Drafts**: Generate screen layouts from text descriptions
- **Smart Rename**: Automatically organize messy layer names
- **Visual Search**: Find similar components and patterns across your files
- **Auto Layout Intelligence**: AI-suggested spacing and alignment
- **Make Designs (Beta)**: Create and iterate on designs with natural language
- **Real-time Collaboration**: Multiple designers working simultaneously

## Who It's For

Product designers, design teams, and cross-functional product squads. Essential for any team that needs collaborative design with developer handoff. The AI features amplify experienced designers and accelerate junior designers.

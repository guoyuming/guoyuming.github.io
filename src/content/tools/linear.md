## What It Does

Linear is a modern project management tool built for product teams who value speed and clarity. It combines issue tracking, project planning, and roadmapping with AI features that automate categorization, detect duplicates, and streamline triage workflows. Everything is keyboard-navigable and blazingly fast.

## Key Features

- **AI Triage**: Automatically categorize, label, and prioritize incoming issues
- **Cycles**: Time-boxed sprints with automatic rollover and velocity tracking
- **Roadmaps**: Visual project timelines for stakeholder communication
- **Slack Integration**: Create issues from messages with full context preserved
- **Duplicate Detection**: AI identifies similar issues to prevent redundant work
- **Keyboard-First**: Navigate and manage everything without touching a mouse

## Who It's For

Product and engineering teams (5-500 people) who want fast, focused project management. Especially suited for software teams tired of bloated tools. Linear rewards teams that want discipline without bureaucracy.

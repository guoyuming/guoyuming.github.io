## What It Does

Notion AI enhances the popular workspace tool with AI capabilities woven into every surface. Ask questions across your entire workspace, generate and edit content, autofill database properties, and get summaries of any page. It turns scattered information into a connected, searchable knowledge base.

## Key Features

- **Workspace Q&A**: Ask questions and get answers from your entire Notion workspace
- **AI Writing**: Draft, edit, summarize, and translate content inline
- **Database Autofill**: AI-powered property generation for databases
- **Meeting Summaries**: Turn notes into structured action items
- **Connected Search**: Find information across all your team docs instantly
- **Templates with AI**: Pre-built workflows with AI automation built in

## Who It's For

Teams that live in Notion for documentation, project management, and knowledge sharing. The AI add-on is most valuable for organizations with large knowledge bases where finding and synthesizing information is a daily challenge.

## What It Does

Perplexity is an AI research engine that provides direct, cited answers to complex questions. Unlike traditional search engines, it synthesizes information from multiple sources and presents structured responses with inline citations you can verify. Pro Search conducts multi-step research with follow-up questions.

## Key Features

- **Cited Answers**: Every claim linked to its source for verification
- **Pro Search**: Multi-step deep research with clarifying questions
- **Spaces**: Collaborative research collections for teams
- **Focus Modes**: Academic, writing, math, video, and social filters
- **API Access**: Integrate search capabilities into your own tools
- **File Analysis**: Upload PDFs, images, and documents for analysis

## Who It's For

Product managers, researchers, analysts, and anyone who needs to quickly gather and synthesize reliable information. Replaces the Google-then-read-ten-articles workflow with direct, sourced answers.
